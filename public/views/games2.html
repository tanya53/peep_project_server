Gather the Tumblers

The peeps frequently go out riding at night on ursula looking for tumblers. They wanted to show you how hard it is to grab them and not get hit by a nuppe. Everytime you get a tumbler you become smaller so it is easier to move around, when you get small enough you will advance to a new level, there are four levels. You use the arrow key move yourself around the nightsky.
