Nibbles

There are no snakes on peep, so the peeps have no fear of snakes. They have learned to control the motion of the snake (use the arrow keys) and feed them atomic fireballs (orbs of hot red candy) to keep them from trying to sample a peep. Unfortunately the atomic fireballs make them grow and at times the sugar high has them running into the wall or themselves, which will cause them to pass out. The purple pest has invaded level three to annoy the snakes. Puprple pests were stowaways on the peepmobile, the peeps consider them to be nuisance, but they can immobilize the snake if they hit it in the head. The peeps are worried that the human species would be terrified if the pests become more wide spread and the peeps are worried their covert operation would be discovered.
