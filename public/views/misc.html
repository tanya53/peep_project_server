Your Weather

Are there any peeps near you? Just take a gander at the map.

The peeps are keeping track of each other by marking their current location on google maps. As you can see the peeps can move rapidly, considering that the peep mobiles travel faster then the speed of light. They also keep track of the location of the newly landed stardust tumblers (known to humans as micrometeorites). They need to make sure they sweep in and gather them up before the scientists from such places as the Imperial College of London accidently collect them in their search for micrometeorites and alien life forms.

Mission statement: To indoctrinate the human race into peepism using a wide assortment of methods including but not limited to fake news, game addiction, social psychology, and the generation of behavioral algorithms.
